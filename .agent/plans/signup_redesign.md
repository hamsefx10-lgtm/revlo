# Qorshaha Casriyeynta Bogga Is-diiwaangelinta (Signup Modernization Plan)

## 1. Hadafka (Objective)
Inaan bogga Signup-ka ka dhigno mid **Casri ah (Modern)**, **Fudud (User Friendly)**, oo leh **Muuqaal Qurux badan (Premium UI)**, si loo kordhiyo tirada dadka is-diiwaangeliya (Conversion Rate).

## 2. Qaybaha Wax Laga Bedelayo (Key Areas)

### A. Naqshadda Guud (Layout & Structure)
- **Status**: Hadda waa 50/50 (Form Bidix, Sawir Midig). Waa qaab fiican.
- **Qorshaha**: Waan sii qurxinaynaa. Qaybta sawirka (Right Side) waxaan ku dari doonaa **"Testimonials Slideshow"** ama **"Feature Highlights"** oo dul saaran 3D background-ka, si macmiilku u arko qiimaha (Value) inta uusan is-diiwaangelin.

### B. Foomka & Meelaha Wax Lagu Qoro (Input Fields)
- **Status**: Inputs caadi ah oo leh Border.
- **Qorshaha**: Waxaan u bedelaynaa **"Modern Floating Inputs"** ama **"Glassmorphism Style"** oo aad u nadiif ah.
- **Validation**: Marka qofku si sax ah u qoro Email-ka ama Password-ka, waxaan soo saaraynaa calaamad "Check" cagaaran oo degdeg ah (Real-time Feedback).

### C. Xulashada Qorshaha (Plan Selection)
- **Status**: Waan casriyeynay (Grid 2x2, Icons, Description). **(DONE)**.

### D. Badhanka "Signup" (Call to Action)
- **Qorshaha**: Waxaan ka dhigaynaa mid weyn, Gradient leh, oo leh animate fudud marka la dul istaago (Hover Effect).

### E. Social Proof (Kalsooni)
- **Qorshaha**: Waxaan ku daraynaa qayb yar oo hoose oo leh: *"Ku biir 500+ Shirkadood"*, si qofku ugu dhiirado.

## 3. Tallaabooyinka Xiga (Next Steps)
1. **Hagaaji Input Fields-ka**: Ka dhig kuwo casri ah oo leh focus-ring qurux badan.
2. **Kordhi Quruxda Header-ka**: "Bilow Safarkaaga" ka dhig mid soo jiidasho leh.
3. **Ku dar Testimonials/Highlights**: Qaybta Midig (Right Side).
